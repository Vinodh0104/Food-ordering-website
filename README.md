# Smart Restaurant Ordering System using QR Code

A professional web-based restaurant management system designed to simplify food ordering, reduce manual work, and improve customer experience using QR Code technology.

This project solves common restaurant problems such as time wastage in taking orders, delayed billing, and lack of visibility for new restaurants. Customers can scan a QR code placed on the table, access the restaurant menu instantly, place orders directly from their mobile phones, and submit feedback after dining.

The system helps restaurant owners manage orders efficiently while improving customer satisfaction through a faster and contactless ordering experience.

## Problem Statement

Traditional restaurant ordering systems often create delays due to manual order taking, billing issues, and staff dependency. New restaurants also struggle to gain recognition and customer trust.

This project provides a digital solution where customers can directly place orders using QR code scanning, reducing waiting time and improving operational efficiency.

---

## Solution Provided

The Smart Restaurant Ordering System introduces a QR-based ordering workflow where:

1. Customers scan a QR code placed on the restaurant table
2. They are redirected to the restaurant website
3. They browse the food menu
4. Orders are placed directly from mobile devices
5. Restaurant staff process the order instantly
6. Customers provide ratings and reviews after dining

This creates a faster, smarter, and more professional restaurant management process.

---

## Complete Workflow

### Step 1 — Customer Enters Restaurant

A customer visits the restaurant and sits at a table where a unique QR code is available.

### Step 2 — QR Code Scanning

The customer scans the QR code using their smartphone camera.

### Step 3 — Website Redirection

The QR code redirects the customer to the restaurant ordering website.

### Step 4 — Login / Register / Guest Access

The customer can:

- Login
- Register as a new user
- Continue as a Guest User

Registered users may receive discount offers.

### Step 5 — Menu Browsing

Customers can view:

- Food Name
- Description
- Price
- Food Images

### Step 6 — Add to Cart and Place Order

Customers select food items and place their order directly through the website.

### Step 7 — Database Storage

The order details are stored securely in the MySQL database.

### Step 8 — Restaurant Staff Processing

Restaurant staff receive the order and begin food preparation.

### Step 9 — Billing and Delivery

The order is served and the billing process is completed.

### Step 10 — Feedback and Reviews

Customers can submit:

- Ratings
- Reviews
- Suggestions

This helps restaurants improve service quality and online reputation.
